Why a Security Training ?

In an always evolving cyber security world you want to make sure that your staff is updated with the most recent attacks. Our training courses will provide:

  • Live hacking demo.
  • Access to our training material (slides + pdfs).
  • Access to our labs to practice attacks.

Proelians do not provide Cyber Security packages, Proalians deliver them. Our audience varies from non-technical to technical staff. We customise our training material to fit your requirements and needs. Our hands-on labs allow you to practice your skill in a real-world and up to date environment.
